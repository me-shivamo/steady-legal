STEADY — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

STEADY ("the app", "we") is an AI-powered calorie and nutrition tracking app for Android and iOS, operated by Shivam. Contact: shivambhawsar.next@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and sign-in provider (email/password or Google).
  • Health & fitness data you enter: date of birth, sex, height, weight and weight history, goal weight, activity level, dietary restrictions, calorie/macro/water goals, food and drink logs, water intake, body measurements, and your messages to the in-app AI coach.
  • Meal photos: photos you take or select to log meals.
  • Usage analytics: app events (e.g. sign-in, onboarding steps, meal logged), associated with your account ID and email.

How we use your data

  • To provide the service: logging meals, computing nutrition, tracking progress, and personalising your calorie and macro targets.
  • To analyse your meal descriptions and photos with AI so they can be converted into nutrition data.
  • To understand how the app is used and improve it (analytics).

We do not sell your data and we do not show ads.

Who processes your data

We use the following service providers, acting on our instructions, to run STEADY:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and photo storage. All of the data listed above is stored here, protected by per-user access rules.
  • OpenRouter / OpenAI models — when you log a meal by text or photo, the meal description and/or photo (plus relevant context such as your goals and today's totals) is sent to AI models to identify foods and estimate portions. This data is used only to produce your nutrition results.
  • USDA FoodData Central — food names only are sent to look up verified nutrition data. No personal information is included.
  • PostHog — usage analytics, associated with your account ID and email.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your data is deleted immediately and permanently (see below).

Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account at any time inside the app: Settings → Delete account. This permanently erases your profile, food logs, photos, weight and water history, measurements, and chat history. You can also request deletion without the app — see our account deletion page.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS). Database access is restricted with row-level security so each account can only ever read its own data.

Children

STEADY is not directed at children and is intended for users aged 18 and over.

Not medical advice

Calorie and nutrition figures in STEADY are AI-assisted estimates for general wellness purposes. They are not medical advice — consult a qualified professional for medical or dietary decisions.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
